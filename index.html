About


Ever since I was a student in Business, Strategy and Data has been my passion. I still remember making it print "Hello, Daniel" in python for the first time. As I went through the basic tutorial, it was almost magic, being able to get the computer to do whatever I wanted, just by typing in these funny little symbols, limited only by my own imagination and ingenuity. Throughout of the last years, I launched on to any ideas for projects I could get, moving from " Hello World", all the way to a Data Science. I learned Relational Databases,SQL, Python, R, JIRA, BI Tools, Excel, Git, Then Tableau in online courses I could find on anything remotely related to Data Science.
This passion carried me through my education. Here there were more resources available to me than I ever could have imagined. I studied new languages, algorithms, data visualizations, product experimentations, regressions, linking data and analytics to business strategy and operations to drive real impact.
So, now, in industry, this passion remains with me. There's certainly yet more to learn, yet more problems to solve, and yet more to analyze. And for this, I am very grateful.
